Class for calculating the equation of motion using Lagrange (symbolic), only second order systems possible !!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!Obsolete. Please do not use anymore.!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

  • q (list of lists of sympy.Function (q depends on time q(t)) [[q1,dq1,ddq1],[q2,dq2,ddq2],...]) – generalized coordinate of the system and the derivatives

  • U (sympy.Add or sympy.Mul) – potential energy

  • F (list of sympy.Add, sympy.Mul or int/float if the force is constant) – external force (1-dim force) e.g. constant force or damper
